# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to make participation in the **PhantomCollect** community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, technical ability, or background.

---

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Using sexualized or violent language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or harassment.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and may remove or edit contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
